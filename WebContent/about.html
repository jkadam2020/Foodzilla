About us

"We are online take-away food ordering marketplace"

We are online take-away food ordering marketplace,
focusing on emerging markets. Foodzilla group is
in its start-up phase. Our main goal is to make
restaurants available online and provide them with
an efficient software and technology to generate
additional demand. For consumers, Foodzilla offers
the convenience to order food online with wide variety,
from which they can choose their favourite meal.
